How to Invest with ENVAI

ENVAI is a blockchain-based platform enabling secure, transparent investments in asset-backed real estate projects. Follow these simple steps to start investing with ENVAI:

Step 1: Get a Crypto Wallet. Before buying tokens, investors will need to get a crypto wallet.

Step 2: Choose the project you want to invest in (Investment Type)

Step 3: Choose the amount you want to invest in the specific project.

Step 4: Enter your valid Wallet Adresse

Step 5: Pay with Crypto or FIAT as per your convinience!
