Process

b. Here's how it works:

i. Property Pooling: A group of commercial properties is pooled together.

ii. Tokenization: The pooled properties are divided into fractional shares, each represented by a security token.

iii. Token Sale: These tokens are sold to investors through an STO, raising capital for the investment fund.

iv. Investment and Management: The funds raised from the token sale will be used to acquire and manage commercial properties.

v. Income Distribution: Property income generated, such as rental income and capital appreciation, is distributed to token holders based on their ownership percentage.

Benifits

c. Benefits of Investing in Tokenized Real Estate:

i. Fractional Ownership: Investors can access high-value commercial real estate with smaller investments.

ii. Increased Liquidity: Tokens can be traded on secondary markets, offering more liquidity than traditional real estate investments.

iii. Transparency: Blockchain technology ensures ownership, transactions, and revenue distribution transparency.

iv. Reduced Transaction Costs: Smart contracts automate many processes, reducing administrative costs.

v. Diversification: Investing in a pool of properties can diversify risk.

vi. Professional Management: Experienced real estate professionals manage the properties.

vii. Economic Potential: By tokenizing commercial real estate, investors can gain exposure to various properties and potentially earn returns from rental income and property appreciation.
