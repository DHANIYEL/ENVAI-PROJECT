Types of Projects

ENVAI offers a variety of project types in real estate and buildings

1. Office Buildings

Modern, energy-efficient office buildings are being developed to cater to the growing demand from local and international businesses.

2. Shopping Malls

Large-scale shopping malls with a mix of international and local brands are being built in major cities and tourist destinations.

3. Hotels and Resorts

Luxury hotels and resorts are being constructed in popular tourist destinations

4. Industrial Parks and Warehouses

Strategic industrial parks and warehouses are being developed to support the growing manufacturing and logistics sectors.

5. Apartments

Investing in apartment complexes can be a lucrative venture, offering stable rental income, potential for appreciation, and tax benefits.

6. Mixed-use

Developments combine residential, commercial, and retail components and offer diverse amenities and services

7. Airports

Bangui M'Poko International Airport is located seven kilometers northwest of Bangui, the capital of the Central African Republic. It is slated for renovation.

8. Data Centers

Investing in data centers offers several benefits, including consistent rental income due to the high demand for data storage and processing power, exposure to the rapidly growing digital economy, and the potential for diversification in an investment portfolio.

9. Resort Properties

These include all-inclusive resorts, boutique hotels, wellness retreats, golf resorts, and historical and cultural resorts.
