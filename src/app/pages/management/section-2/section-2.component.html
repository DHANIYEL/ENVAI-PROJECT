Management Team

Meet our management team at the helm of ENVAI

Tino Herold

Technology Affairs

Tino Herold is a seasoned international business professional with a diverse background in finance, real estate, and renewable energy. Born and initially educated in Karl-Marx-Stadt (now Chemnitz), Germany, Tino's career has spanned multiple industries and geographies, reflecting his adaptability and deep expertise.

Tino began his career with a strong educational foundation, completing vocational training in Mechanics for Data Processing Machines at ASCOTA AG, followed by engineering studies at Chemnitz University. He later supplemented this with bank studies at the IHK/Deutschen Bank AG, equipping him with a unique blend of technical and financial knowledge.

His professional journey commenced with roles at Deutsche Bank AG and CETAG AG, where he served as Branch Manager and Vice President of the Board, respectively. Between 2003 and 2008, Tino specialized in real estate finance, holding positions at B2B Creditweb Deutschland GmbH / GMAC RFC and B/F/S GmbH Berlin. His international career took off in 2009 when he became Sales Director at New York Community Bank.

Since 2010, Tino has been at the helm of iSAP Solutions, serving as Director across multiple regions, including London, Dubai, Russia, Ukraine, and Toronto. His leadership has been instrumental in the company's growth and expansion into diverse markets. In 2020, he embarked on a new venture, "Falconic," demonstrating his entrepreneurial spirit and innovative mindset.

Tino's linguistic prowess—he is fluent in English, proficient in Russian, and has a good grasp of Italian and basic French and Arabic—has significantly contributed to his success in international business. His specialized knowledge in banking and payment systems, trade finance, the renewable energy sector, and management and accounting in SMEs, coupled with his intercultural knowledge of the MENA region, makes him a valuable asset in any multinational setting.

Throughout his career, Tino Herold has demonstrated exceptional leadership, adaptability, and a commitment to driving business growth. His diverse experiences and skills make him a respected figure in the international business community.

Prof. Andreas Maass

Legal Affairs

Prof. Andreas Maass is a distinguished legal professional and academic with an impressive career spanning over three decades. Born and raised in Germany, his journey is marked by a commitment to excellence, service, and education.

Andreas began his academic pursuits at the Städtisches Gymnasium Sundern, where he completed his Abitur in 1985. Following his high school education, he served in the German Federal Armed Forces from 1985 to 1987, rising to the rank of lieutenant in the reserve. His legal education commenced in 1987 at the University of Münster, culminating in the first state examination in law at the Higher Regional Court of Hamm in 1993.

Andreas continued his legal training with a clerkship at the Regional Court in Erfurt from 1993 to 1995, successfully completing his second state law examination at the Thuringia Judicial Examination Office in 1995. Since 1996, he has been a practicing lawyer, bringing his extensive knowledge and experience to various legal arenas.

In addition to his legal practice, Andreas has made significant contributions to the academic world. Since 2008, he has served as the Head of the Legal Department at Perfect Timing Holding, showcasing his expertise in corporate law and governance. In 2021, he assumed the role of Vice President at ATAFOM University, further cementing his commitment to education and academic leadership.

Prof. Andreas Maass is known for his dedication to legal education and his ability to bridge the gap between academic theory and practical application. His career is a testament to his unwavering commitment to the legal profession and his passion for sharing knowledge with future generations of legal professionals.

Prof. David Grzan

Investment Affairs

David Grzan, MBA/MSFS, is an accomplished Wall Street executive with over 25 years of experience in investment banking, private equity, commercial banking, fintech, and commercial real estate. David is actively packaging project sponsors and sourcing alternative capital to fund significant humanitarian and other projects, including energy, infrastructure, residential and commercial real estate, mining, transportation, telecommunications, healthcare, utilities, digital assets, and commodities worldwide.

David has held numerous C-suite and board positions, including roles at Beacon Capital Management Services, Remada Company, Equity Bank, Rochester Medical Technologies, Carlson CRE Group, New Horizon Kids Quest, and PreIPO Corporation. He has a proven track record in leading strategic planning, business development, and financial management initiatives. Notably, he played a key role in Equity Bank's turnaround and received recognition from the FDIC for his contributions.

David's expertise encompasses financial engineering, capital sourcing, and deal structuring, which enables him to enhance businesses' bankability and enterprise value. He has secured over $2 billion in off-balance-sheet financing for Fortune 200 companies, highlighting his proficiency in complex financial transactions. Additionally, he has served as a fractional C-suite executive, providing strategic guidance to for-profit and non-profit organizations. He has also held FINRA-registered licenses, including Series 7, 63, and 66.

A strong advocate for financial education, David serves as the Dean and Professor at ATAFOM University International, where he inspires and empowers the next generation of financial professionals. Additionally, he is a member of the Forbes Finance Council. He holds an MBA in Banking from the University of London (SOAS), an MSFS from the Denver School of Financial Planning, and a BA in Business Administration from Long Island University. His motto, "Dictum Meum Pactum—My Word Is My Bond," highlights his commitment to integrity and excellence.

Prof. Dr. David Nightingale

Compliance Affairs

Dr. David Thomas Nightingale is an accomplished executive with a stellar career traversing the finance, petrochemical, oil, gas, and banking industries. With a solid educational foundation from esteemed institutions such as Liverpool University, Durham/Teesside University, and Syracuse University, culminating in a Ph.D. in International Finance and Economics from the United Nations Academy, David has continually pioneered innovative solutions in his fields of expertise.

His professional journey commenced in Houston, Texas, where he revolutionized multi-dimensional modelling paradigms and machine learning in supercomputer MPP Geology analytical AI platforms within the petrochemical financial services sector. David's acumen led him to advise several US government agencies, including the Senate Intelligence Oversight Committee and the US State Department, on critical IT and satellite remote sensing projects, notably contributing to the Pan Am 103 investigation.

In the banking sector, David has held pivotal roles at prominent institutions like the Royal Bank of Scotland, BBVA Bank, and ING Bank. He has spearheaded initiatives in financial risk modelling, compliance, and data architecture, implementing cutting-edge stochastic modelling techniques and heuristic bond valuation algorithms. His work has significantly influenced global regulatory frameworks such as Basel III, IFRS9, and GDPR, enhancing capital adequacy and risk evaluation procedures.

David's expertise extends to advising Fortune 500 companies and top global banks, where he has directed projects of national security importance and developed advanced financial models for AML and fraud detection. His recent endeavors include consulting on energy and green resource analysis, and banking and finance business intelligence, incorporating MIFID, Basel IV, and blockchain/crypto applications. David's illustrious career and unparalleled expertise have earned him profound respect throughout the global financial community.
